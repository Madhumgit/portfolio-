Hall of Fame & Swags

1

In a proactive security engagement, I identified and reported a critical vulnerability (CVSS Score: 10.0) in the infrastructure of Flipkart - one of India's largest and most trusted digital commerce ecosystems. This issue, if left unaddressed, had the potential to compromise sensitive systems across platforms like Myntra, Flipkart Wholesale, Flipkart Health+, Cleartrip, and ANS Commerce. The discovery earned me a place in Flipkart’s Hall of Fame at first position, recognizing the impact and importance of responsible disclosure in securing enterprise-level ecosystems.

The Flipkart Group is one of India's leading digital commerce entities and includes group companies Flipkart, Myntra, Flipkart Wholesale, Flipkart Health+, Cleartrip, and ANS Commerce.
